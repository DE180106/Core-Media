LOGO

OVERVIEW

Media Core’s mark is a minimalist red triangle nested within a balanced black and white wordmark—an immediate nod to the universal “play” icon, instantly signaling motion and digital media.

STRUCTURE

The triangle sits to the left of the logotype, its apex aligned with the cap height of the letters; stroke weights and negative space follow a strict 1×1 geometric grid to preserve clarity at any size.

SLOGAN

The Vietnamese tagline appears beneath the wordmark in Montserrat Medium, letter spaced +10 pt, echoing the triangle’s base width for perfect visual harmony.

CLEAR SPACE

Reserve clearance equal to the height of the triangle on all sides; no graphic, text, or page edge may breach this zone to maintain legibility and brand impact.

PLACEMENT LOCK UP

Use the horizontal lock up (triangle + wordmark + slogan) as default; vertical or centered variants are allowed only when horizontal space is constrained.

MISUSES

Never rotate, recolor, outline, distort, or place the logo over busy imagery; avoid gradient fills, drop shadows, or substituting the triangle with any other shape.

COLOR

NEGATIVE AND POSITIVE COLOR

Deploy the red triangle and black wordmark on white for positive usage; invert to a white logo on solid red or black backgrounds for negative applications.

COLOR PALLETE

Media Core adopts a focused and strategic color system to ensure consistency, impact, and brand recognition across all media applications.

ADDITIONAL COLOR

While Media Core maintains a minimalist core palette, select additional colors may be introduced to enhance functionality and adaptability—especially in digital and multimedia contexts.

TYPOGRAPHY

PRIMARY FONT

Used exclusively in the logo, Montserrat Medium reinforces Media Core’s bold, modern character with geometric clarity and strong presence. It is not applied to body text or other applications beyond brand identity.

SECONDARY FONT

Its rounded curves and open spacing bring a friendly, approachable feel while maintaining a sleek, contemporary look. It ensures readability in both print and digital environments, complementing the logo without overpowering it.
